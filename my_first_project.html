CAREER BEGINNINGS

In 2014, Tatiana released her first EP entitled Heartstrings. It featured five original songs written and performed by Tatiana herself. The EP was well-received and was played on radio stations all over the world. She followed up with her second EP entitled Speak Now in 2015. This was followed by her debut full-length album LoveLife in 2016. The album featured fan favorites such as “Like You” and “Helplessly”.
